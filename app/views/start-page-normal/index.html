Get early conciliation or a certificate for an employment tribunal

Use this form to get:

  • early conciliation - where Acas speaks with you and your employer separately to try and resolve the problem without going to an employment tribunal
  • a certificate to make a claim to an employment tribunal

You will need the name and address of the organisation or employer - find this on a recent letter, payslip, contract, P60 or P45.

The form will automatically save so you can return to it if you need to.
